## FAQ

<details class="faq-item">
<summary>What is handled.sh?</summary>
<p>A personal productivity assistant that lives in your WhatsApp and takes real actions for you. Send a message, and it handles the rest: tasks, emails, calendar, price tracking, reminders, and more. Telegram is also supported.</p>
</details>

<details class="faq-item">
<summary>How do I get started?</summary>
<p><a href="https://app.handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">Sign in</a> with Google or Microsoft and connect WhatsApp. Your assistant is ready in under a minute. You can also connect Telegram, Gmail, Calendar, Todoist, and more from the dashboard.</p>
</details>

<details class="faq-item">
<summary>How is this different from a chatbot?</summary>
<p>A regular chatbot answers questions inside a chat window. handled.sh connects to your calendar, email, and task apps, takes action on your behalf, and proactively reaches out with reminders, alerts, and daily summaries. It runs in your own private environment, not a shared service.</p>
</details>

<details class="faq-item">
<summary>Is my data private?</summary>
<p>Yes. Unlike shared cloud services, every user gets their own isolated container. Your data never touches another user's environment. We don't sell your data, don't train on your conversations, and don't track your behavior. <a href="/security">Learn more.</a></p>
</details>
