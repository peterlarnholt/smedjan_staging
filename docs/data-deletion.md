# Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

## How to delete your data

1. Sign in to your dashboard at [https://handled.sh/dashboard](https://handled.sh/dashboard)
2. Click **Delete account** at the bottom of the page
3. Confirm by typing DELETE when prompted

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your user profile (name, email, profile picture)
- Your assistant container and all its data (conversation history, memory, workspace files, scheduled jobs)
- All connected integration tokens (Google, Microsoft, Todoist)
- Your WhatsApp phone number and configuration
- Your Telegram bot token and configuration
- Your Stripe subscription (cancelled immediately)

Deletion completes within minutes and cannot be undone.

## Disconnect individual services

You can also disconnect individual integrations without deleting your account:

1. Sign in to your [dashboard](https://handled.sh/dashboard)
2. Click **Disconnect** next to the integration you want to remove

Disconnecting an integration immediately deletes its stored tokens.

## Contact us

If you cannot access your dashboard or need assistance with data deletion, contact us at [contact@handled.sh](mailto:contact@handled.sh) and we will process your request within 30 days.
