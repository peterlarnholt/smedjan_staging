## Frequently Asked Questions

<div id="getting-started" class="faq-category">

### Getting Started

<details>
<summary>What is handled.sh?</summary>
<p>handled.sh is your personal productivity assistant, available on WhatsApp and Telegram. It can manage your calendar, send emails, track prices, set reminders, track prices, monitor flights, and manage reminders — all through a simple chat message.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I sign up?</summary>
<p>Visit <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">handled.sh</a> and sign in with your Google or Microsoft account. Your assistant is ready instantly — no installation required.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I connect WhatsApp?</summary>
<p>After signing in, go to your <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">dashboard</a> and tap "Connect" on the WhatsApp card. You'll receive a verification code — just send it to our WhatsApp number and you're connected automatically.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I connect Telegram?</summary>
<p>From your <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">dashboard</a>, tap "Connect" on the Telegram card. You'll need to create a bot via <strong>@BotFather</strong> on Telegram and enter the bot token and your user ID. Step-by-step instructions are shown on the dashboard.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Do I need both WhatsApp and Telegram?</summary>
<p>No — you only need one. Connect whichever app you prefer. You can always add the other one later.</p>
</details>

</div>

<div id="integrations" class="faq-category">

### Integrations

<details>
<summary>What integrations are available?</summary>
<p>Currently supported: Google Calendar, Microsoft Calendar, Gmail (send), Outlook (read & send), Todoist, and outbound email via Resend. More integrations are coming soon.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I connect my calendar?</summary>
<p>Go to your <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">dashboard</a> and tap "Connect" on Google Calendar or Microsoft Calendar. You'll be redirected to authorize access. Once connected, your assistant can view, create, and manage events.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I connect my email?</summary>
<p>From the <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">dashboard</a>, connect Gmail (for sending emails) or Outlook (for reading and sending). You'll be redirected to Google or Microsoft to authorize access.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I connect Todoist?</summary>
<p>Tap "Connect" on the Todoist card in your <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">dashboard</a>. You'll be redirected to Todoist to authorize access. Once connected, your assistant can create, complete, and manage your tasks.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I disconnect an integration?</summary>
<p>Yes. Go to your dashboard, and tap "Disconnect" on any connected integration. You can reconnect at any time.</p>
</details>

</div>

<div id="features" class="faq-category">

### Features

<details>
<summary>What can my assistant do?</summary>
<p>Your assistant can manage your calendar, create and complete tasks, send emails, set reminders, track prices, look up flights, send files, and monitor websites for changes.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do reminders work?</summary>
<p>Just tell your assistant something like "Remind me to call Mom at 5pm" or "Every Monday morning, send me a summary of my week." It handles one-time and recurring reminders automatically.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can it send emails?</summary>
<p>Yes — if you've connected Gmail, Outlook, or enabled outbound email on the dashboard, your assistant can draft and send emails on your behalf.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can it look up information for me?</summary>
<p>Yes. Your assistant can check prices, track deliveries, monitor websites for changes, look up flights, and summarize emails for you.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can it send me files?</summary>
<p>Yes. Your assistant can create and send files — images, documents, spreadsheets, and more — directly in your chat.</p>
</details>

</div>

<div id="privacy" class="faq-category">

### Privacy & Security

<details>
<summary>Is my data private?</summary>
<p>Yes. Every user gets their own completely isolated environment. Your data, conversations, and credentials are never shared with other users.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What does "isolated container" mean?</summary>
<p>Your assistant runs in its own separate workspace — like having your own private computer. No other user can access your files, messages, or integrations.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is handled.sh GDPR compliant?</summary>
<p>Yes. We follow strict European data protection standards. Your data stays yours — we don't sell it, and we never train models on your conversations.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How are my credentials stored?</summary>
<p>All integration credentials (calendar tokens, email tokens, etc.) are encrypted and stored per-account with minimum permissions. We only request the access needed for each integration to work.</p>
</details>

</div>

<div id="billing" class="faq-category">

### Billing

<details>
<summary>How much does it cost?</summary>
<p>The free plan is free forever — unlimited tasks and all integrations included. Pro is <strong>$1 for your first month</strong>, then <strong>$10/month or $79/year</strong>. All features, no surprises.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is handled.sh free?</summary>
<p>Yes! The free plan is yours forever — unlimited tasks and all integrations included. No credit card required. Upgrade to Pro for $1 your first month, then $10/mo or $79/yr for unlimited messages.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I cancel?</summary>
<p>You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from the dashboard. Tap "Manage" on your subscription banner, and you'll be taken to the billing portal.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What's the difference between Free and Pro?</summary>
<p>Free gives you unlimited tasks and all integrations. Pro gives you unlimited messages at full power. Your data and integrations carry over when you upgrade.</p>
</details>

</div>

<div id="troubleshooting" class="faq-category">

### Troubleshooting

<details>
<summary>My bot is not responding</summary>
<p>Try restarting your assistant from the <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">dashboard</a> using the "Restart" button. If the issue persists, disconnect and reconnect your messaging app.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>My integration stopped working</summary>
<p>Integration tokens can expire. Go to your <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">dashboard</a>, disconnect the integration, and reconnect it. This refreshes the authorization.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Messages are delayed</summary>
<p>Short delays (a few seconds) are normal — your assistant is thinking and working on your request. If delays are consistently long, try restarting your assistant from the dashboard.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>I'm not receiving reminders</summary>
<p>Make sure your messaging app (WhatsApp or Telegram) is connected and showing as "Online" on the dashboard. Also check that your assistant knows your timezone — you can tell it or send a location pin.</p>
</details>

</div>

## Guides

<div class="guide-card" id="guide-getting-started">
<h3>Getting Started</h3>
<ol>
<li><strong>Sign in</strong> — Visit <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">handled.sh</a> and sign in with Google or Microsoft.</li>
<li><strong>Connect a messaging app</strong> — Choose WhatsApp or Telegram from your dashboard and follow the setup steps.</li>
<li><strong>Send your first message</strong> — Open WhatsApp or Telegram and say hi to your assistant. Try something like "What can you do?" to see its capabilities.</li>
</ol>
</div>

<div class="guide-card" id="guide-integrations">
<h3>Connecting Integrations</h3>
<ol>
<li><strong>Open your dashboard</strong> — Go to <a href="https://handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">handled.sh</a>.</li>
<li><strong>Find the integration</strong> — Scroll to the Apps & Integrations section.</li>
<li><strong>Tap Connect</strong> — You'll be redirected to the service (Google, Microsoft, Todoist) to authorize access.</li>
<li><strong>Start using it</strong> — Once connected, just ask your assistant. For example: "What's on my calendar today?" or "Add milk to my shopping list."</li>
</ol>
</div>

<div class="guide-card" id="guide-reminders">
<h3>Reminders & Scheduling</h3>
<ol>
<li><strong>Set a reminder</strong> — Tell your assistant: "Remind me to call the dentist tomorrow at 10am."</li>
<li><strong>Set a recurring reminder</strong> — Say: "Every weekday at 9am, tell me my schedule for the day."</li>
<li><strong>Check your reminders</strong> — Ask: "What reminders do I have?" to see what's scheduled.</li>
<li><strong>Cancel a reminder</strong> — Say: "Cancel the dentist reminder" and your assistant will remove it.</li>
</ol>
<p class="guide-note">Your assistant needs to know your timezone to schedule correctly. Tell it your city or send a location pin in the chat.</p>
</div>
