## FAQ

<details>
<summary>How do I sign up?</summary>
<p><a href="https://app.handled.sh/dashboard" target="_self">Sign in</a> with Google or Microsoft. Your productivity assistant is set up automatically. Then connect WhatsApp, Telegram, Gmail, Google Calendar, Todoist, whatever you use, from the dashboard.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is handled.sh really free?</summary>
<p>Yes. The free plan gives you unlimited tasks and all integrations. No credit card required, no time limit.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What does the $1 first month include?</summary>
<p>Full Pro access for $1 your first month — unlimited messages at full power. After that it's $10/month or $79/year. Cancel anytime.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What happens if I cancel Pro?</summary>
<p>You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period, then you're moved to the free plan. Your data, conversations, and connected apps are all preserved.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How does billing work?</summary>
<p>Pro is $1 for the first month, then $10/month or $79/year, billed monthly through Stripe. You can cancel anytime from your dashboard. No emails, no phone calls.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is my data private?</summary>
<p>Yes. Every user gets their own isolated space. We don't sell your data, don't train on your conversations, and only request minimal permissions from connected apps.</p>
</details>
