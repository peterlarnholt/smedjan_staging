Do I need to install an app?
No. Handled works entirely within WhatsApp. No downloads, no new apps, no learning curve.
Can Handled replace a real executive assistant?
For most day-to-day scheduling, reminders, and email triage — yes. Handled manages your calendar, sets reminders, drafts emails, and keeps your tasks organised. It won't make coffee, but it handles the admin.

Absolutely. All messages are protected by WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption. Handled is a META verified Business Partner, meeting Meta's strict security and privacy standards.
