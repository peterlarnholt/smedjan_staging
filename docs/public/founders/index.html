FAQ

Questions? Answered.

What is Handled?+

Handled is your AI intern that lives in WhatsApp. It handles tasks, reminders, notes, and automation without requiring you to install or switch between multiple apps. Just chat naturally and get things done.

How is this different from ChatGPT on WhatsApp?+

Most chatbots just answer questions. Handled is a productivity assistant that takes action: creates tasks, sets reminders, manages your calendar, sends emails, and integrates with your tools. It's built specifically for getting work done, not just chatting.

Do I need to install an app?+

No. Handled works entirely within WhatsApp. If you already use WhatsApp, you're ready to start. No downloads, no new apps, no learning curve.

Is it secure?+

Yes. All messages are encrypted by WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption. Handled is a META verified Business Partner, meaning it meets Meta's strict security and privacy standards.

How much does it cost?+

Handled is free forever with 5 messages per day and all core features. Pro is $1 for the first month, then $79/year with unlimited messages, priority support, and early access to new features.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Pro is annual but you can cancel anytime. Free plan is always free with no commitments.

What integrations do you support?+

Gmail, Google Calendar, Notion, Todoist, and more. We're adding new integrations every week based on user requests.
