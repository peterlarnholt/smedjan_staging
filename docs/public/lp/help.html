Getting Started

How do I start using Handled?

  1. Click "Start Free" or visit handled.sh/whatsapp
  2. Open the WhatsApp conversation with Handled
  3. Send a message to activate your AI intern
  4. Start creating tasks, reminders, and notes immediately

That's it! No signup forms, no passwords, no downloads.

What can I ask my AI intern to do?

Your AI intern can handle many productivity tasks:

  • Tasks: "Create task to finish report by Friday"
  • Reminders: "Remind me to call John at 3pm"
  • Notes: "Save this idea for later: [your note]"
  • Calendar: "Schedule meeting with Sarah tomorrow at 10am"
  • Lists: "Add milk to grocery list"
  • Quick info: "What's my schedule today?"

Common Questions

Do I need to download an app?

No! Handled works entirely within WhatsApp. If you have WhatsApp, you're ready to use Handled.

Is Handled free?

Yes! The free plan includes unlimited tasks, reminders, and notes. Pro plan ($79/year) adds advanced features like priority support and premium integrations.

How is my data protected?

All messages are encrypted by WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption. Handled is a META verified Business Partner. We never sell your data. Learn more on our Security page.

Can I use Handled on multiple devices?

Yes! Since Handled works through WhatsApp, you can access it on any device where you use WhatsApp (phone, desktop, web).

Troubleshooting

My AI intern isn't responding

Try these steps:

  1. Check your WhatsApp connection
  2. Send a simple message like "Hello"
  3. Wait 30 seconds (processing may take a moment)
  4. Restart WhatsApp if still not responding

If issue persists, contact hello@handled.sh

A reminder didn't trigger

Possible causes:

  • Timezone mismatch (check your timezone settings)
  • Ambiguous time ("tomorrow afternoon" vs "tomorrow 2pm")
  • WhatsApp notifications disabled

For critical reminders, use specific times: "Remind me at 3:00pm"

How do I delete my data?

Send this message to your AI intern:

"Delete my account"

Or email hello@handled.sh and we'll delete everything within 30 days.
