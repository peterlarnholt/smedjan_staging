1. Information We Collect

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI productivity services
  • Process your requests (create tasks, set reminders, manage notes)
  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features (anonymized)
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Train public AI models on your private conversations

If Handled is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy
