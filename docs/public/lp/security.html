Security & Privacy

Your data security is our top priority. Here's how we protect you.

META Verified Business Partner

End-to-End Encryption

Handled operates entirely within WhatsApp, which means all your messages benefit from WhatsApp's industry-leading end-to-end encryption. Your conversations with your AI intern are encrypted from your device to our servers—no one else can read them, not even us.

What this means for you:
Every task, reminder, note, and message is protected by the same encryption that secures your personal WhatsApp chats.

META Verified Partner

Handled is a META verified Business Partner. This verification means:

  • We meet Meta's strict security standards
  • We undergo regular security audits
  • We comply with Meta's privacy policies
  • We maintain high reliability and uptime

Data Handling Practices

What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide our service:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number (for authentication)
  • Messages you send to your AI intern (to process your requests)
  • Tasks, reminders, and notes you create
  • Usage analytics (anonymized)

What We Do With Your Data

  • Process your requests (create tasks, set reminders, etc.)
  • Improve our AI models (with your permission)
  • Provide customer support when you contact us
  • Comply with legal requirements

What We DON'T Do

  • We never sell your data. Period.
  • We don't share your data with advertisers
  • We don't use your data for targeted advertising
  • We don't train public AI models on your private data

GDPR Compliance

Handled is fully GDPR compliant. If you're in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your data (request a copy anytime)
  • Delete your data (we'll erase everything)
  • Export your data (portable format)
  • Correct your data (fix any inaccuracies)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@handled.sh.

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as you use Handled:

  • Active users: data retained indefinitely
  • Inactive users (90+ days): we may delete data after notification
  • Deleted accounts: all data erased within 30 days

Security Measures

  • All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Automated backup systems
  • 24/7 security monitoring

Incident Response

If we ever experience a security incident that affects your data, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours
  • Explain what happened and what data was affected
  • Detail the steps we're taking to prevent future incidents
  • Provide guidance on protecting your account

Questions?

If you have security concerns or questions, contact us at hello@handled.sh. We take security seriously and respond to all inquiries.
