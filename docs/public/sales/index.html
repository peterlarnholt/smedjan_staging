FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Does Handled replace my CRM?

No. Handled works alongside your CRM. It captures follow-ups, meetings, and action items from WhatsApp so your reps don't have to manually log everything. Think of it as the fast lane into your sales workflow.

How does Handled help with follow-ups?

Just tell Handled in WhatsApp: "Remind me to follow up with Priya at Acme in 3 days." It sets the reminder and pings you when it's time. You can also ask "What follow-ups are overdue?" to see everything in one place.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All messages are protected by WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption. Handled is a META verified Business Partner, meeting Meta's strict security and privacy standards.
