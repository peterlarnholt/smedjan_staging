## How It Works Under the Hood

<div class="arch-list">

<div class="arch-item">
<div class="arch-num">1</div>
<div class="arch-text">
<strong>You message your productivity assistant</strong> on WhatsApp or Telegram. The message is sent over the platform's own encrypted channel.
</div>
</div>

<div class="arch-item">
<div class="arch-num">2</div>
<div class="arch-text">
<strong>Your isolated space picks it up.</strong> No other user's data touches your environment. Completely separate.
</div>
</div>

<div class="arch-item">
<div class="arch-num">3</div>
<div class="arch-text">
<strong>The system processes your request.</strong> Your message is not stored or used for training. Ever.
</div>
</div>

<div class="arch-item">
<div class="arch-num">4</div>
<div class="arch-text">
<strong>If needed, your productivity assistant accesses connected apps</strong> (Calendar, Gmail, Todoist) using encrypted connections with minimum permissions.
</div>
</div>

<div class="arch-item">
<div class="arch-num">5</div>
<div class="arch-text">
<strong>The response comes back to you</strong> on WhatsApp or Telegram. Nothing is shared, leaked, or sold. Ever.
</div>
</div>
